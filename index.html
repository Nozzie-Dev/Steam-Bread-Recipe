Steam Bread Recipe

You will need:

  • Butter, for greasing
  • 4-6 Porcelain Sipper Cups
  • 1 Large Bowl
  • 1 Large Pot

Ingredients:

  • 4 1/2 cups cake flour
  • 2 tsp instant dry yeast
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 4 tsp sugar
  • 2 cups warm water

Instructions:

  1. Grease 4-6 porcelain sipper cups with butter.
  2. Sift dry ingredients into a large bowl.
  3. Add warm water.
  4. Knead until the dough combines and does not stick to your hands.
  5. Cover with cling wrap, put in a warm place and allow dough to rise for 25 minutes.
  6. Knead dough again for 2 minutes, cover with cling wrap and allow to rise for 15 minutes.
  7. Knead the dough for 2 minutes and separate into the sipper cups.
  8. In a large pot, boil enough water reach halfway up the cup(when the water boils it must not reach dough or get in the cup)
  9. Place cups in the pot. Cover with lid, on medium heat, steam the bread for at least 45mins or until cooked
  10. Monitor water levels, top up with boiling water if neccesary. To test if bread is cooked insert a knife in the centre. When bread is ready, it should come out dry.
